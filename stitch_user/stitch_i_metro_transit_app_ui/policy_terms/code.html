01

Terms of Service

By accessing or using I-Metro, you agree to be bound by these Terms of Service. Our services include the I-Metro mobile application, digital ticketing systems, and station-side connectivity tools.

Core User Obligations

  • check_circle You must maintain a valid digital or physical ticket at all times within I-Metro transit zones.
  • check_circle Digital credentials are non-transferable and tied to your verified biometric or device profile.
02

Privacy & Data Security

Our data philosophy is built on the principle of "Minimal Kinetic Footprint." We only collect data necessary to facilitate your journey and optimize station flow.

Geolocation

Used exclusively for station proximity triggers and real-time route optimization. No long-term history is stored unless explicitly opted-in.

Payments

Processed through encrypted tokenization. I-Metro never stores your raw financial credentials on our local servers.

03

Usage Guidelines

I-Metro is a shared digital ecosystem. Users are expected to interact with our kiosks and mobile interfaces with reasonable care.

"The efficiency of the city depends on the collective integrity of its travelers."

Automated bot interactions, reverse-engineering of ticketing APIs, or attempts to spoof GPS coordinates are strictly prohibited and will result in permanent account suspension.

Need more clarity? Our support team is available 24/7.
